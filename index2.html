Jonas Walfridsson

Speech therapist and lecturer with expertise in dyscalculia

I am a speech therapist with expertise in the assessment of mathematical difficulties/dyscalculia and have been working with these assessments at Danderyd Hospital since 2013. I am passionate about spreading knowledge about numerical and mathematical difficulties, including dyscalculia, and am a frequently sought-after lecturer. I have also had the privilege of participating in features on the topic in both SVT and TV4. I stay constantly updated on the research landscape regarding numerical and mathematical difficulties, including dyscalculia.


Do you need a lecturer in dyscalculia?

I am happy to participate in forums to spread knowledge or discuss numerical and mathematical difficulties, including dyscalculia. I give lectures on-site and digitally in Swedish and English.
